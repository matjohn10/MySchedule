# MySchedule
Tkinter scheduling app for school. You can add up to 6 courses and add assignments that are specific to those courses.
## Files Information
The 'Schedule_Interface.py' file runs the application. The txt files are used as databases (will be updated to SQL databases) and the 'User.py' file contains the logic behind the users courses and assignments.

## How To Use the App
First run the 'Schedule_Interface.py' file.
### Login and Signup Page

The creation of your account is simple. While signing up, your username will be checker for its uniqueness. During login, write your username and your password. If you forgot your password, you can use your username to access it.
### First Time Loging In
&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; <img width="399" alt="Screen Shot 2022-11-12 at 1 02 09 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/117942546/201488276-a4344674-cb46-442f-b20b-878729e7dd67.png">
At your first login, a form will ask you about your school, level of education, and area of study. Afterwards, you will be taken to your schedule page.
### Schedule Page
On this page, you can add courses (up to 6), display your timetable, open your assignment page, and drop your courses.
